Questions we get a lot.

Do I need to RSVP or register?

No. Just show up.

Is it free?

Yes, coffee and conversation are both free.

Do I need to be a developer or AI expert to attend?

No, we're all just trying to figure it out these days. Come and learn!

Can I present my work?

Come to a meeting first. See if it's something you're interested in. If you are, we can talk there.

What about parking?

Like most of Midtown, parking ain't free. There's plenty of street parking at 8:30am on a Monday. The Midtown MARTA station is also a couple blocks away.

How long has this been going on?

We've been meeting weekly since 2025.
