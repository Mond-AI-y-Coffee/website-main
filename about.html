Mond(AI)y Coffee

The community behind the meetup.

Mond(AI)y Coffee started with a simple idea: that the best thinking happens over a good cup of coffee. Every Monday morning in Atlanta, builders, founders, researchers, and curious minds gather at ATDC to share what they're working on, swap ideas, and figure out what AI can actually do in the real world — not just in theory.

There's no agenda, no keynotes, no vendor pitches. Just people who are genuinely excited about building with AI and generous enough to show their work. Some are deep in production systems. Some are just getting started. All are welcome.

If you're in Atlanta and want to be part of it, reach out to Adam at Adam.Gautsch@atdc.org.
