Show Up

The easiest way to get involved is to just come on a Monday. No RSVP, no registration — just show up at 8:30am at ATDC and grab a cup.

Every meeting is a little different. Sometimes there's a structured presentation, sometimes it's an open conversation. Either way, you'll leave with new ideas and new contacts. First-timers are always welcome.

When Mondays, 8:30 – 10:00 am
Where ATDC · 75 5th St NW, Suite 2230, Atlanta, GA 30308

Join the Community

We organize the community through GitHub. Joining the Mond-AI-y-Coffee organization lets you stay connected, see what members are building, and contribute to community projects.

Membership is reviewed and approved by the organizers. To request access, visit our GitHub organization and click Request to join — or reach out directly to Adam.Gautsch@atdc.org.
