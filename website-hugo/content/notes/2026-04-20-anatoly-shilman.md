# Pre-Read
[Toly Shilman](speakers/anatoly-shilman/), CEO and Co-Founder of [Cogbias AI ](https://www.cogbias.ai/) is presenting. CogBias is building the decision integrity layer for enterprises and academia.    

## About Cogbias AI:  
[Cogbias](https://cogbias.ai/) AI helps revenue teams make better decisions by eliminating the cognitive biases that distort how they process information. We analyze AI outputs, research, sales conversations, and messaging to detect and correct biases — like confirmation bias and anchoring effects — before they influence how deals are won or lost. The result is cleaner, more reliable decision-making without replacing the tools teams already use.  
  
## About the Tech:  
We build our product using a modern PERN stack (PostgreSQL, Express, React, and Node.js), with React also powering our browser extension interface. Our AI infrastructure is supported by a Flask-based API, with models developed in Python and deployed via Google Cloud. For collaboration and development workflow, we rely on Jira for project management and GitHub for version control. We also integrate AI-assisted tools such as GitHub Copilot and ChatGPT to accelerate development and streamline coding tasks, while maintaining a human-in-the-center approach to ensure code quality and thoughtful system design. Currently, our application is hosted using Porkbun and Render, with plans to further scale and expand our infrastructure within Google Cloud as the product grows.
